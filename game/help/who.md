# WHO

`who` lists connected players, their connection time, and their recent idle
time. You can give the first part of a player's name to narrow the list:

```text
who Alex
```

Players hidden from WHO are omitted when the server is configured to hide
them. Wizards can use `@who` to see the privileged connection details.
